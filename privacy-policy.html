Privacy Policy

Focus Blocker

Last updated: 2025-12-28

1. Introduction

Focus Blocker respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or share personal data.

2. Data Collection

Focus Blocker does not collect any personal data.

  • No accounts
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No advertising IDs
  • No third-party SDKs

All app data (settings, focus sessions) is stored locally on your device.

3. Accessibility Service Usage

Focus Blocker uses the Android Accessibility Service solely to provide its core functionality: blocking selected apps during focus sessions.

Specifically, Accessibility is used to:

  • detect which app is currently in the foreground
  • prevent access to apps selected for blocking

The app:

  • does not record screen content
  • does not store UI data
  • does not transmit accessibility data
  • does not monitor user behavior beyond app blocking

The Accessibility Service is not used for advertising, analytics, or tracking purposes. Accessibility data is processed only in real time and never leaves the device.

4. Data Sharing

Focus Blocker does not share data with third parties. No data is transmitted to servers because no servers are used.

5. Children’s Privacy

Focus Blocker does not knowingly collect data from children. Because no personal data is collected, there is no risk of child data misuse.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be published at the same URL.
