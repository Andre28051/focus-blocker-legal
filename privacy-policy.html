Privacy Policy

Focus Blocker

Last updated: 2026-02-11

1. Introduction

Focus Blocker is designed with privacy in mind. The app operates locally on your device and does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

2. Data Collection

Focus Blocker does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information.

  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party advertising SDKs

All app settings, schedules, and focus session data are stored locally on your device. No user data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Usage Access Permission

Focus Blocker uses the Android Usage Access permission (Usage Stats) to detect which application is currently in the foreground.

This is required solely to determine whether a selected app should be blocked during an active focus session.

The app:

  • does not store detailed app usage history
  • does not analyze user behavior
  • does not transmit usage data
  • processes usage information only in real time

All processing happens locally on your device.

4. Display Over Other Apps (Overlay Permission)

Focus Blocker uses the “Display over other apps” (overlay) permission to show a blocking screen above apps that are restricted during a focus session.

The overlay is used only to enforce blocking functionality. It does not capture screen content, record interactions, or monitor activity outside of blocking logic.

5. Data Sharing

Focus Blocker does not share any data with third parties. No personal data is transmitted because the app does not use remote servers.

6. Support Communication

If you voluntarily contact support (for example via email), you may choose to provide diagnostic information to help resolve your issue.

This may include:

  • App version
  • Android version
  • Device model
  • Battery optimization status
  • Premium or Free status
  • Selected blocking mode (e.g. hard or soft blocking)

This information is used only to provide technical support and is not used for analytics or marketing purposes.

7. Children’s Privacy

Focus Blocker does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected automatically, there is no profiling or behavioral tracking.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be published at the same URL.
